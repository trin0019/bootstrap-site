Global Warming

The gradual increase of the Earth’s temperature, due primarily to the variety of increase in fuel consumption in human activities. This increase in temperature poses a significant threat to the Earth by the way it is changing weather patterns as well as endangering animals and is influencing extinction. As the temperature continues to rise some of the most notable impacts have already begun to occur. Ice is melting in the glaciers; Sea levels are rising, migration of animals to cooler climates and the increased amount of precipitation.

Other effects that could happen should temperatures continue to rise include: continual increase in Sea levels, intensities of storms would increase and less accessible sources for fresh water. Without proper action to reduce global warming, the Earth’s temperature will continue to rise and increase the number of threats to ecosystems in our planet.
